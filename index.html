Problem Statement

Manual Attendance Is Holding Schools Back

Traditional attendance and leave management creates inefficiencies that impact both staff and administrators.

Time Consuming

Manual roll calls and paper-based attendance sheets waste valuable teaching time every day.

Prone to Human Error

Manual data entry leads to mistakes in attendance records and reporting inaccuracies.

Proxy & Buddy Punching

Without identity verification, colleagues can sign in on behalf of absent staff members.

What Sets eHadir@SMKBN Apart

Anti-Buddy Punching

Integrated Platform
